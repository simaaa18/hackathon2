Impact Trips

We’re living in a deprived world, where many people live in poverty, but not just in terms of material resources but of psychological, relational and spiritual terms. Many people battles with hopelessness, isolation, shame and low self worth.

 

On the other hand, God’s heart is to see His children empowered to be hopeful people, living in community, and knowing our worth in His eyes. As His creation and beloved disciples, we’re called to bring ‘Shalom’ to our society. ‘Shalom is more than just the absence of conflict, but signifies complete harmony and flourishing (Isaiah 11.6-9). God wants us to be The Salt and The Light to this broken world.

Travel for Change

Organized together with our partners, impact travel creates opportunities for meaningful and enjoyable interactions, and allows travelers to contribute to projects that benefit local communities. We achieve this through collaboration with local groups, social entrepreneurs and non-governmental organizations to participate in community development and economic empowerment projects.

SOCIAL EDUCATION & ENTREPRENEURSHIP FOR CHILDREN

EMPOWERING WOMEN THROUGH CRAFT

EARTH HEIR® works with over 100 artisans from women’s cooperatives, indigenous tribes, and refugee groups, across six states of Malaysia. By combining traditional artisanal skills with modern, contemporary design, Earth Heir is a true celebration of Malaysia’s varied heritage art forms and unique cultural narratives.
